The logical study of quantity, shape, and change.

Mathematics is a fundamental discipline built on logic, abstract thought, reasoning, and the study of patterns. It uses numbers, equations, variables, and formulas to model and solve real-world problems involving quantity, structure, space, and change.

Branches Of Math

  1. Arithmetic

    • Basic number operations.

  2. Algebra

    • Symbols representing unknown numbers.

  3. geometry

    • Shapes, sizes, properties.

  4. Trignometry

    • Triangle angle and side relationships.

  5. Calculus

    • Studies continuous change.

  6. Statistics

    • Data collection and analysis.
